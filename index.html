Varela Digital is a digital scholarly edition and knowledge website dedicated to the correspondence preserved in the Varela Collection, documenting political, military, and social networks during the Farroupilha Revolution (1835–1845).

The pilot corpus is based on the letters published in Volume II of the Anais do Arquivo Histórico do Rio Grande do Sul. All documents are encoded in TEI–XML and enriched through structured metadata, standoff annotation, and linked data modeling.

What you can explore

  • Critical transcriptions of letters and related documents
  • Rich correspondence metadata (sender, addressee, place, date)
  • Named entities and semantic relations extracted from the texts
  • A reproducible editorial workflow from TEI to HTML and RDF
