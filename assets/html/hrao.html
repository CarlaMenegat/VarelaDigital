Implementation notes

The HRAO vocabulary documentation is automatically generated from its Turtle declaration using a lightweight Python pipeline based on RDFLib and Jinja2. The approach is conceptually inspired by ontology documentation tools such as Widoco, while remaining fully transparent, reproducible, and project-specific.

Domain: http://xmlns.com/foaf/0.1/Person

Range: http://xmlns.com/foaf/0.1/Person

Semantics: symmetric property.

Represents non-biological, non-marital kinship created through ritual, social, or customary bonds such as compadrio (godparenthood), ritual parenthood, fraternity bonds or other historically relevant forms of fictive kinship. The property is symmetric because these relations are mutually acknowledged in historical records.
